Three-minute Training

【37】You can learn about business in your daily life. You do not need to be a rich adult to understand it. You just need to look around you. Peter Lynch was a famous business man. He had a simple but great idea. He said, "Look at what you know." Think about your school and your friends. What do they like? Is there a new video game everyone plays? Is there a new burger shop with a very long line? These are very important signs. Adults who work with money look at difficult papers and numbers. They often do not know what young people like. But you know. You know what is cool right now. If a shop is always busy, maybe that company is doing well. If a new snack is popular, maybe that company will grow big. So, please open your eyes. Look at your snacks, your clothes, and your games. You can find great ideas everywhere. You can understand business just by looking at your world.
